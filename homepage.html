About the Toolkit

This toolkit for resistance is both a call for peace and a plea. While building the toolkit and doing research into past movements calling for peace, I was heavily drawn to the flower child movement of the 1960s, and wanted to put a transactional spin on the motif of flowers by making the flower pot pictured feel like an offering, however, the invocation of a hypnosis spiral to give the piece a more sinister aspect. It is practically a given for citizens in this world to feel incredibly helpless while few world leaders cause mass destruction in the name of their political goals, to the point where it feels the only solution is quite literal hypnosis.
