Instructions

How to Build the Sign

  • The poster is most effective posted on a surface instead of handheld- a wall, telphone pole, back of a car/bus/etc., due to its size.
  • However, if it is handheld, I recommend taping the top left and right corners to opposite ends of the cardboard tube to make it easiest to carry as well as avoid creasing and wrinkling the poster.

How to Recyle Your Tube

  • Cardboard tubes are easily biodegradable! They compost into soil in approximately 2-3 months, so they should be recycled.
  • The most important thing to remember is to remove the plastic ends of the tube when you recycle the tube. The plastic ends are not biodegradable.
